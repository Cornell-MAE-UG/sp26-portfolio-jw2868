As part of a class assignment, I was tasked with designing a nutcracker.

This is how I solved the problem:

Find: I have to find a design a nutcracker, the usabability, and ultimately a modified version using a linear actuator

Given: For my assumptions I assumed that the nut laid rest on length L1, while L2 was where the distributed force of the hand ultimately is. Along with design assumptions, I made assumptions to a human's max grips strength which was 103lb, average nut diameter of 1 inch, and a force required to crack a nut of 490 lb. 

Now that I made these assumptions, I began to diagram my nutcracker and drew a free body diagram which showed the lengths, members, and location of forces.
